About Me

I am an applied mathematician working in quantitative systems pharmacology (QSP) and pharmacometrics. I use mathematical, statistical, and computational tools to advance the pre-clinical and clinical development of new therapeutics.

  • Secretary, SIAM Life Sciences Activity Group, 2024–
  • Principal Scientist, Modelling and Simulation, AbCellera, 2023–
  • Scientist II, Modelling and Simulation, Notch Therapeutics, 2021–2023
  • NSERC Postdoctoral Research Fellow, 2018–2021
    Department of Physics and Astronomy, Vanderbilt University
