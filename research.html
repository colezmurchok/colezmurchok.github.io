Research

The central goal of my research is to develop, analyze, and simulate mathematical models to unravel the interactions that govern biological systems. I use a variety of approaches depending on the biological context: PDEs, ODEs, agent-based models, stochastic simulations, and physics-based models. I value working collaboratively, and seek to work with others whenever possible. My research sits at the interface between applied mathematics and biology—I use models to test biological hypotheses in silico and to generate biological predictions that can be experimentally verified.
